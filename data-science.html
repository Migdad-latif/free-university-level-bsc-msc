Open MSc FinTech Specialist + Final Project
The FinTech-pathway compulsory and optional modules: Blockchain Programming (DSM070), Financial Markets (DSM080), Neural Networks (DSM150), NLP (DSM140), and the MSc Final Project (DSM500).
5 modules · ~18 hrs/week · 105 credits
How to Use This Curriculum
📅
Follow the sequence
Modules are ordered by dependency. Don't skip Year 1 — it underpins everything in Year 2 and 3.
🔗
Use the links
Every resource is free and auditable. Prioritise the starred (★) resources in each module first.
🏗️
Build projects
Each module has a capstone mini-project. These form your GitHub portfolio for MSc applications.
📝
Document everything
Write blog posts or Jupyter notebooks explaining what you learned. Imperial/UCL love demonstrable evidence.
